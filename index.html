About

Amir Noori is an Assistant Professor in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the Azad University of Karaj. His research focuses on power systems, optimization, and AI applications in energy networks. He is actively involved in collaborative projects on large-scale power system dynamics and digital transformation.
Room M341, ECE Building Azad University
